Welcome To Niantic!

Niantic Connecticut is a beach town located on the eastern coast. It's a small villiage and only has 3,114 occupants. That is, until the summer comes and there are loads of people lining the small beaches and hitting the local fast food businesses. Niantic has many local stores and beaches to occupy the tourists for the few months of summer they manage to come to the coast. The locals run everything from pizza places, to candy shops. Niantic is the perfect summer destination when looking for a happy town, ready to welcome anyone.

Meet the business of Niantic!

Niantic has an array of businesses that can keep you busy in the summer. Most all of them being located on Niantic's main street. Talking a walk down Main Street you'll see some of these shops,

  • Frosty Treat
  • Gumdrops and Lollipops
  • Tumbleweeds
  • Niantic Cinemas
  • Family Pizza
  • Cafe Sol
  • Grace

And these are only a few local shops of the many located on Main Street!

The Beaches of Niantic!

You may be asking yourself, how many beaches in Niantic could there be? Well there's a lot. For the small town you have your fair share of options!

  1. Macooks Beach
  2. Cresent Beach
  3. Hole in the Wall Beach
  4. Cini Beach
  5. Old Black Point Beach
  6. Attawan Beach
  7. Rocky Neck beach
  8. Giants Neck Beach
  9. Niatnic Beach

And these aren't all of them! Don't forget if you are a member of any beach association in old black point, you have your very own beach at the end of your road! But no lifeguards are on duty ever! And if you go there, everyone that's a local despise you, the choice is yours!
